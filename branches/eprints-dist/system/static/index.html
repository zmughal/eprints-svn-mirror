Welcome to this generic EPrint server, running the default configuration for EPrints archive software. More information is available about the archive and open archiving in general.

Archive Viewing Services

Browse by Subject

This option lets you browse the subject hierarchy and see what papers are in each subject category.

Search the Archive

A simple search will let you search for author, title, publication and year, and advanced search will let you search more fields too.

Registration-based Services

Before you can use the subscription service, or deposit papers to the archive, you'll need to register with us. No charge is made for registering or for the use of any service on this archive.

 

Registration Information

Select this option for instructions on how to register, be reminded of your username and/or password if you've forgotten, and inform us if your e-mail address has changed.

  

Subscriptions

This feature allows you to instruct the archive to automatically e-mail you with details of new eprints appearing in the archive. You can choose which subject areas you are interested in, and how often you wish to be informed of updates: Every day, week or month. Requires user registration.

Deposit Papers

Select this option to access the archive author area. From this area you can update your personal details, deposit new papers, and remove and update existing papers from the archive. Requires user registration.
