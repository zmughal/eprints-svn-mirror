TITLE: eprints.org: Mailing List

Mailing Lists

We currently have two mailing lists, described below. In addition you may be interested in the OAI mailing lists - see the Open Archives Initiative pages.

eprints-announce

If you want to be informed when new versions of the eprints software become available, then you can join the eprints.org announcement mailing list.

The mailing list will be a very low-volume list and will be used for no other purpose than to inform recipients of the availability of the further revisions of the eprints.org software, and any major revisions of the eprints.org website. Only authorised users can mail to the list; it cannot be used as a discussion list.

eprints-tech

This is the technical discussion list for people who are using eprints and/or are interested in the technical side of eprints.org - getting it working, customising it and new directions for the next version.

The technical discussion list is archived online.

Subscribing

To subscribe to either list, send a mail with the following to the following as the message body:

subscribe eprints-announce

or

subscribe eprints-tech

Send the mail to: majordomo@ecs.soton.ac.uk

You will be mailed and asked to reply with confirmation.
