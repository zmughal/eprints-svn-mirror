TITLE: Registrations

In order to access some areas of the archive, you'll need a user registration. This can be a reader registration, or an author registration. Note that no charge is made for registering with us or using any of our services.

Reader registrations Author registrations
These allow you to set up a subscription, which tells the archive software to automatically email you with new additions to the archive meeting your criteria every day, week or month. If at a later date you decide you want to upgrade to an author registration you can. These allow you to submit papers to the archive, as well as creating a subscription like the reader registration lets you.

To join up

To create a registration, please send a mail to auto@eprints.org with one of the following subject lines:

"join reader" if you wish to join up as a reader

"join author" if you wish to join up as an author

Once you've sent that mail, you'll be mailed back a username and password that you can use to access the relevant areas of this archive.

To upgrade a reader registration

To upgrade your reader registration to an author registration, click here.

If you change your email address

If you already have a registration, and you wish to inform the archive of a change of your email address, send a mail to auto@eprints.org with the subject"change email". Send the from your new email account. The body of your email should contain the following lines:

USERNAME <your user name>
PASSWORD <your password>

The e-mail address associated with your account will be changed to the address you sent the mail from. Changes of address are handled this way so that we can be sure that we have a valid e-mail address for you. You will be sent a mail informing you of any changes successfully made.

If you've forgotten your username or password

The archive can remind you if you've forgotten your username and/or password. Just click here and type in your e-mail address (the one associated with your account.)
