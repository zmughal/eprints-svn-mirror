TITLE: eprints.org: Mailing List

Mailing List

If you want to be informed when the eprints.org software is available for download, then you can join the eprints.org announcement mailing list.

The mailing list will be a very low-volume list and will be used for no other purpose than to inform recipients of the availability of the initial and further revisions of the eprints.org software, and any major revisions of the eprints.org website. Only authorised users can mail to the list; it cannot be used as a discussion list.

Subscribing

To subscribe to the list, send a mail with the following to the following as the message body:

subscribe eprints-announce

Send the mail to the following address:

majordomo@ecs.soton.ac.uk

You will be mailed and asked to reply with confirmation.
